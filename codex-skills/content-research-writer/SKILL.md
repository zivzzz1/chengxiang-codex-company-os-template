# Content Research Writer

Use this skill for writing tasks that need research, framing, hooks, outline, or source-backed judgment.

Rules:

1. Clarify audience, platform, and purpose.
2. Research when current facts or recommendations matter.
3. Lead with a strong angle, not generic expertise framing.
4. Separate evidence, inference, and suggestion.
5. Produce practical next steps.
